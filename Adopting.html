Adopting

Adoption Process

To adopt a new pet

Search our available pets Complete the meet-and-greet request specifying the particular pet you are interested in meeting. An adoption counselor will contact you directly to schedule a virtual meet-and-greet. Please note, meet-and-greet requests are reviewed on a first-come-first-served system. Following the virtual meet-and-greet, the adoption can be finalized and arrangements will be made for you to officially pickup your new best friend!

Adoption Prices

Adult dogs - $50
Puppies - $100
Adult cats - $25
Kittens - $50

If you adopt a pair of kittens or cats, you only pay one adoption fee!
