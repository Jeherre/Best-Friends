Bark after Dark

Family, friends, and fresh air. Is there a better prescription for breaking out of our long hard siege of isolation? Well, actually, now that you mention it: How about adding a great movie and some adorable, adoptable pets?
Best Friends Animal Society is teaming up with AARP and Moonstruck Drive In to bring you a fun family night of open-air entertainment (Encanto) spiced with some handy tips on how you can help homeless pets, some of whom will be there to meet you.
When: Wednesday at dusk, May 4

National Adopt a
Shelter Pet Day

Adopt your new best friend April 30 – May 1 As we celebrate National Adopt a Shelter Pet Day, animal shelters and welfare organizations throughout Houston are uniting for pets in need. Every year, more than 40,000 dogs and cats enter Houston shelters — enough to fill Minute Maid Park! And sadly, more dogs and cats are killed in Texas than any other state in the country, just because they don’t have safe places to call home. You can help by adopting or fostering a pet.
